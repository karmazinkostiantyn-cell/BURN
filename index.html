WELCOME TO BURN

Boosting University Psychological Resilience and Wellbeing
in (Post-) War Ukrainian Nation (BURN)

PROJECT DURATION

  • Duration: 24 months
  • Start: 01 Jan 2024
  • Finish: 31 Dec 2026

PROJECT GOALS

  • Professionalize crisis psychology at university by introducing new courses
  • Improve psychological service provided by universities
  • Establish Centers for Psychological Resilience and Wellbeing
  • Disseminate the experience

TARGET GROUPS

  • Psychology students
  • University psychological service workers
  • Ukrainian HEI members

BURN PARTNERS

Universities from Ukraine, Germany, Latvia and Estonia participate in the project
