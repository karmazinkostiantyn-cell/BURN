PhD module "Working with Refugees and Survivors of War" taught in English (0,5 ECTS)

← Back to Courses

The module “Working with Refugees and Survivors of War” is designed for doctoral students. It will be integrated into a course in pedagogy or psychology and will be mandatory for all doctoral students. The module covers theoretical approaches to working with refugees and war victims and provides opportunities for developing relevant practical skills. The module aims to develop resilience, first aid skills, and the ability to create emotionally safe environments when working with groups of people affected by war in the students.
