BA course "Crisis Online Counseling" taught in Ukrainian (3 ECTS)

← Back to Results

The course provides a comprehensive understanding of the theoretical foundations of psychological crises and their impact on individuals. It offers an in-depth view of the nuances of remote psychological counseling for people in crisis, exploring the use of various online resources, their advantages, and limitations in crisis counseling practice. Additionally, the course develops the ability to establish effective counselling contact between a psychologist and a client in online settings, while also equipping students with practical skills in applying modern psychological techniques for online crisis counseling at various stages.
